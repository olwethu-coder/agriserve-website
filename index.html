ABOUT US

AgriServe Consulting is a consulting company formed in 2021, with a sound background in the regulatory requirements of the South African regulatory authority, thus providing expert regulatory advisory and support to the South African Plant Science Industry.

As agriculture continues to play a pivotal role in the nation's economy, navigating the complex regulatory landscape is crucial for businesses involved in plant protection.

With over 15 years of experience in regulatory affairs, government, and stakeholder management, we enjoy a clear understanding of the regulatory environment.

OUR MISSION

To provide excellent, high-quality, and value-based expert service offerings in the regulatory and compliance environment for the Plant Science Industry in South Africa.
